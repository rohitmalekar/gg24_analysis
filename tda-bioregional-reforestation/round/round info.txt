### **Funding Mechanism**

**Phase 1: Bioregional Level Allocation (**11/17/25 - 12/30/25**)**

-   Funds from the $100k matching pool will be allocated using a QF-style round ([more about QF - Quadratic Funding](https://www.wtfisqf.com/)) to determine the percentage of funding distributed to each bioregion. Individuals and Organizations will donate to one or more bioregions to signal their support. After the funding round concludes we will match these donations with a portion of the $100k matching fund. This will enable us to crowdfund additional funding for the initiative while also utilizing the community's feedback to distribute funds to the bioregions.

**Phase 2: Project Level Allocation (1/15/26-7/15/26)**

-   Bounties for reforestation are established in each bioregion.
-   Outcome-based funding via Silvi's app: Smart contracts on Celo/Allo for milestone payouts (65% planting, 35% survival).
-   EAS attestations serve as decentralized MRV for proof of planting and survival.
-   Treekipedia AI ensures species selection matches local soil, climate, and biodiversity data.
