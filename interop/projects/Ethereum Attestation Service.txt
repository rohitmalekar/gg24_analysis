Ethereum Attestation Service (EAS) is an infrastructure public good for making attestations onchain or offchain about anything. Open-source, permissionless, token-free. Built by the community for the community. Live on mainnet, several L2s, & testnets.

Attestations are digital signatures on structured data used to build more trust online & onchain.
How do you decide who and what to trust? Our online and onchain worlds are filled with both genuine and deceptive information. We need a common way we can trust the authenticity of information and the safety of our interactions. Anytime you need to prove or verify something, attestations will play a critical role. Learn more about why we exist.
