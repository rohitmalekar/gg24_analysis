### IMPACT STATEMENTS

The Privacy Pool Paymaster makes private transactions on Ethereum more accessible, affordable, and censorship-resistant. By eliminating the dependency on third-party relayers, it advances decentralization and interoperability for privacy protocols. The project serves as a template and public good for the ecosystem --- providing reusable paymaster logic and reference tests for integrating privacy-preserving withdrawals with ERC-4337 wallets. In the long term, it contributes to a more open and privacy-preserving Ethereum, aligning with the cyberpunk goals

### CONTRIBUTIONS

We've built an open-source repository containing ERC-4337 Paymaster contracts and tests that demonstrate Privacy Pool withdrawals using sponsored gas payments --- where users only pay the actual gas cost of the withdrawal action. This implementation demonstrates how decentralized gas sponsorship can replace reliance on centralized relayers in a privacy pool system, utilizing the standardized ERC-4337 Account Abstraction Infrastructure.The same paymaster architecture can be adopted and extended by other privacy projects, such as Railgun or Veil.Cash, to integrate the ERC-4337 Account Abstraction infrastructure, enabling trustless, standardized, and private withdrawals across different ecosystems.
