About Dev3packDev3pack is a global fellowship program leading online technical education for women+ and student developers, especially in the Ethereum ecosystem.

-   1,200 applicants to join the fellowship from 70+ countries and 400+ universities
-   Over 90% of women represented in the fellowship
-   A strong involved community - who want to learn, build real projects, and contribute to OSS
-   Teaching available in English and Spanish (soon in French)

![Dev3pack Global](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sdaav/zk-privacy/main/de3pack-global.png)About ZK & Privacy BootcampWe launched a 7-week online bootcamp about ZK & privacy for women and student developers, including teachers from PSE, Aztec, zkVerify, Nillion, Starknet and Mina. All the content is open-source, available on Youtube, and can be re-used for creating your own cohort. All sessions are designed with some hands-on practices.![Cover](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sdaav/zk-privacy/main/Cover%20(1).png)

-   Session 1 - Foundations of Cryptography
-   Session 2 - Essential Tools of Modern Cryptography
-   Session 3 - ZKP Basics
-   Session 4 - Advanced ZKP Systems
-   Session 5 - Introduction to Circom and ZK application architecture
-   Session 6 - Introduction to Semaphore Protocol
-   Session 7 - Introduction to Aztec
-   Session 8 - Introduction to Noir
-   Session 9 - Introduction to zkVerify
-   Session 10 - Use Cases and Implementation of zkVerify
-   Session 11 - User Owned Private Data
-   Session 12 - Private AI

For 2026, will create another cohort where teachers will come entirely from the dev3pack community. We got a lot of interest for this first cohort, and wanted to select a small group of people (50 developers) to ensure program quality.After this bootcamp, these developers will get access to the dev3pack ZK & privacy builder club to collaborate on open-source projects related to privacy.

### IMPACT STATEMENTS

-   180+ applicants for the bootcamp (we had to close applications one week before official deadline, because so many great applicants)
-   Selected 50 developers curious about privacy (many already contributed to open-source projects from Ethereum, AAVE, Nethermind, and other projects in the space)
-   90% minimum of women represented, to provide a safe space to learn
-   Participants are giving many great feedback after each session - leading us to 70K+ views about the bootcamp
