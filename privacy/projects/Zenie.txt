Introduction
------------

Zenie is a decentralized escrow protocol enabling trustless peer-to-peer fiat-to-crypto exchanges using zero-knowledge proofs of bank transfers. Users can buy crypto with cash while preserving privacy - no KYC, no centralized exchange, no intermediaries.Demo video: https://x.com/qpzmly/status/1938575567588307220

### How It Works

1.  Depositor locks crypto in smart contract escrow (e.g., 1000 USDC)
2.  Buyer signals intent to purchase (e.g., $500 worth)
3.  Buyer sends bank transfer to depositor's account
4.  Buyer generates ZK proof of transfer (via Reclaim Protocol)
5.  Smart contract verifies proof and releases crypto to buyer

### Key Features

-   Zero-Knowledge Privacy - Prove bank transfers without revealing senders' bank accounts.
-   Multi-Chain Deployment - Live on Base, Kaia.
-   Trustless Escrow - Smart contracts hold funds until ZK proof verification
-   Multi-Currency Support - USD, KRW, extensible to any fiat currency
-   Nullifier System - Prevents replay attacks and double-spending
-   Upgradeable Design - UUPS proxy pattern for protocol improvements

2+ billion people globally lack access to reliable crypto on/off-ramps due to:

-   Geographic restrictions and banking limitations

### IMPACT STATEMENTS

### Our Impact

Zenie delivers financial sovereignty through:

1.  Financial Inclusion - Anyone with a bank account can buy/sell crypto, regardless of location or KYC status
2.  Privacy Preservation - ZK proofs ensure transaction validity without revealing banking credentials or personal data
3.  Cost Reduction - Peer-to-peer model reduces fees from 3-7% to under 1%
4.  Censorship Resistance - Decentralized protocol with no central authority to freeze accounts

### Real-World Use Cases

-   Emerging Markets - Users in South Korea, Vietnam, Southeast Asia buying crypto with local bank transfers
-   Privacy-Conscious Traders - Acquiring crypto without KYC surveillance
-   Remittances - Cross-border workers sending crypto home without expensive wire fees
-   Crypto Off-Ramps - Liquidity providers earning yield by converting crypto to fiat
