### IMPACT STATEMENTS

Eppie is still in the prototype stage, but we've already demonstrated tangible progress and early adoption. Our preview desktop app for Windows, Linux, and macOS has attracted over 1,500 active users who test encryption, Proton Mail integration, and early decentralized messaging features. This growing community provides valuable feedback that helps us refine the app before public Testnet launch.

### CONTRIBUTIONS

Eppie contributes to the Ethereum ecosystem by developing open, privacy-preserving communication infrastructure. It introduces a decentralized messaging protocol that connects Ethereum addresses, wallets, and users through encrypted peer-to-peer channels. By enabling wallets to act as secure mailboxes, Eppie expands Ethereum's utility beyond transactions -- into private human-to-human and dApp-to-user communication. Also, Eppie lowers the entry barrier to Web3 by letting anyone create and use an Ethereum address directly from their email app --- bringing new users into the Ethereum ecosystem through everyday communication. All code and specifications are open source and can be reused by other developers to build privacy tools or integrate secure messaging into their apps.
