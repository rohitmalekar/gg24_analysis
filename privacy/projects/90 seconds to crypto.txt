### IMPACT STATEMENTS

My work is 100% public, educational, and open. It is aimed at helping people understand and adopt self-custodial crypto tools. All content is free on YouTube, remixable, and focused on privacy and sovereignty rather than speculation.Over the past 12 months, I've produced 51 videos and 54 shorts. My content initially focused on short-form explainers that has since expanded to long-form explainer and interviews. I have covered public good protocols such as Railgun, Fluidkey, Fileverse, and Zora. Some lifetime numbers from my YouTube:- 190k+ views - 400k+ impressions - 2600+ watch hours - 18000+ subscribersMy content has introduced thousands of viewers to the value of open, privacy-preserving self-custodial tech. I try to spotlight open source builders that rarely receive mainstream visibility; educate new users on how to go about self-custody safely; and reduce misinformation by focusing on legitimate onchain projects. I believe that my content can create a "rising tide lifts all boats" effect, because the more people understand and trust in self-custody/privacy tech, the more it benefits the open source ecosystem by bringing in more users, legitimacy, and funding.Gitcoin/MACI/Privote support would allow me to: - Continue building and expanding my reach in educating the masses on sovereignty and self-custody tech - Produce more privacy-focused content, such as ZKPassport, EF's institutional privacy work, Kohaku, and so much more - Create downloadable educational resources for builders and educatorsMy north star metric: I will produce 100 more videos+shorts for 2026.
