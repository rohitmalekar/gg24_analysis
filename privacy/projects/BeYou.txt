BeYou is a privacy-preserving social platform that enables anonymous communication, feedback, and voting within verified communities. It empowers people to connect, collaborate, and express themselves freely, without revealing who they are. Users can create or join groups based on shared interests such as nationality, age, or gender, as well as or verifiable credentials such as GitHub or Twitter activity, blockchain transactions or participation proofs (e.g., hackathon invites) verified through ZK Email, all without exposing personal data. All proofs are verified on zkVerify, ensuring trust, scalability, and verifiability across communities. This enables users to set their own access criteria and make collective decisions with full authenticity, proving truth without revealing identity.
