### IMPACT STATEMENTS

ZeroLedger makes private crypto payments practical for everyday use. It reduces costs and complexity so freelancers, gig workers, and small businesses in high-inflation or unbanked regions can enjoy p2p payments without exposing their entire financial history. At the same time, it introduces a new model of compliance --- payments remain private, but regulators can verify integrity without requiring full disclosure. This balance between privacy, usability, and compliance can accelerate real-world crypto adoption by making payments both safer and more accessible.
